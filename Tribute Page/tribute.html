Rene Geronimo Favaloro

A great cardiac surgeon and educator

His life

  • 1923 - Born in La Plata, Buenos Aires
  • 1942 - Admitted into the School of Medicine at the National University of La Plata.
  • 1945 - Began his medical residency at the Hospital Policlínico San Martín, this brought him into contact with patients for the first time
  • 1949 - Graduated with a medical degree
  • 1951 - He married
  • 1967 - began to consider the possibility of using the saphenous vein in coronary surgery
  • 1970 - published one of his best-known volumes, Surgical Treatment of Coronary Arteriosclerosis.
  • 1975 - Founded the Fundación Favaloro
  • 1980 - established the "Basic Investigation Laboratory" financed with his own money
  • 2000 - dies at the age of 77
